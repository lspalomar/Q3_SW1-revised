One Piece(Ongoing)


Introduction

One Piece, a japanese manga turned into an anime. Talks about the story of Monkey D. Luffy in his journey to find the One piece to become the Pirate King. He meets various new people which could be comrades, enemies or friends.

Synopsis

Monkey D. Luffy meets various comrades like Zoro, Sanji, Nami and etc. While adventuring to find the One Piece eventually reaching the Grand Line and traversing through the Red Line to arrive in the New World. While facing enemies like Doflamingo, Blackbeard and many more.

Summary

Since One Piece is currently ongoing Luffy and his friends are currently in Wano kIngdom fighting two Warlords of the Sea, Big Mom and Kaido.

Monkey D. Luffy

He is the main character of One Piece and possesses the power of the "Gum-Gum Fruit".

Roronoa Zoro

The first person Luffy invited to his crew, which later on he becomes the vice captain. Possesses and practices the "Three-Sword-Style".

Vinsmoke Sanji

Another crewmate of Luffy which he meets in a restaurant in the middle of the sea. He uses a fighting style called "Black Leg"
